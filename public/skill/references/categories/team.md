# Team & Organization / 团队与组织

How team structure and organizational design shape software architecture.

团队结构与组织设计如何塑造软件架构。

**21 frameworks** in this category.

## Frameworks

### Spotify Model / Spotify 模型
- **Slug**: spotify-model
- **Complexity**: advanced
- **Quality**: maintainability, usability
- **Author**: Henrik Kniberg & Anders Ivarsson, 2012
- Organize engineering around autonomous squads grouped into tribes, with cross-cutting chapters and guilds for alignment

### Amazon Two-Pizza Teams / 亚马逊两个披萨团队
- **Slug**: amazon-two-pizza-teams
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: maintainability, scalability
- **Author**: Jeff Bezos, ~2002
- Limit team size to what two pizzas can feed (~6-10 people) to maximize ownership and minimize communication overhead

### Inner Source / 内部开源
- **Slug**: inner-source
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: maintainability, usability
- **Author**: Tim O'Reilly, 2000; formalized by PayPal (Danese Cooper, ~2014)
- Apply open-source development practices within an organization to break down silos and improve code reuse

### Platform Engineering / 平台工程
- **Slug**: platform-engineering
- **Complexity**: advanced
- **Quality**: maintainability, usability, scalability
- **Author**: Evan Bottcher (ThoughtWorks), ~2018; formalized in Team Topologies (Skelton & Pais, 2019)
- Build and operate an internal developer platform as a product, enabling stream-aligned teams to self-serve infrastructure and tooling

### Engineering Ladder / Career Framework / 工程师阶梯 / 职业发展框架
- **Slug**: engineering-ladder
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: maintainability, usability
- **Author**: Multiple origins; notably Rent the Runway (Camille Fournier, ~2015) and early frameworks at Google and Facebook
- Define structured growth paths with clear expectations at each level for individual contributors and engineering managers

### Blameless Postmortems / 无指责事后复盘
- **Slug**: blameless-postmortems
- **Complexity**: beginner
- **Quality**: reliability, maintainability
- **Author**: John Allspaw, 2012; rooted in Sidney Dekker's Just Culture and safety science
- Conduct structured incident reviews focused on systemic learning rather than individual blame

### Architecture Review Board (ARB) / 架构评审委员会
- **Slug**: architecture-review-board
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: maintainability, reliability
- **Author**: Enterprise architecture tradition; modernized by ThoughtWorks Technology Radar approach (~2010)
- A governance body that reviews and guides significant architectural decisions to ensure consistency, quality, and strategic alignment

### Technical Debt Management Framework / 技术债务管理框架
- **Slug**: technical-debt-management-framework
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: maintainability
- **Author**: Ward Cunningham, 1992 (debt metaphor); Martin Fowler, 2009 (Technical Debt Quadrant)

### Developer Experience (DevEx) Framework / 开发者体验框架
- **Slug**: developer-experience-framework
- **Complexity**: intermediate
- **Quality**: usability, maintainability
- **Author**: Nicole Forsgren, Margaret-Anne Storey & Chandra Maddila, 2023

### Mob/Ensemble Programming / 群体编程 / 集合编程

- **Complexity**: beginner
- **Quality**: maintainability, usability
